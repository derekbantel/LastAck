The Protocol

How LastAck ensures your message is delivered.

  • Phase 1
    Routine Check-In
    You acknowledge your existence via UI or API. The dead man timer resets.
  • Phase 2
    Grace Period
    You miss a deadline. LastAck enters "Warning" state and notifies YOU privately to cancel the alarm.
  • Phase 3
    Trigger Execution
    Silence confirmed. Emails sent, webhooks fired, archives unlocked.
